About This Project

"Privacy isn't about something to hide. Privacy is about something to protect."

Today's encryption will be broken by quantum computers. Intelligence agencies are already harvesting encrypted data, waiting for quantum capabilities to decrypt it later. Your private communications today may be readable in 10-20 years.

Quantum is Phexora AI's research initiative building cryptographic protocols that remain secure even against future quantum attacks. We're not waiting for the threat to arrive - we're preparing now.

True privacy requires more than encryption - it requires privacy by default. Zero-knowledge proofs allow you to prove statements without revealing the underlying data. You can prove you're eligible without showing why. You can prove compliance without exposing details. This is the foundation of financial sovereignty and individual freedom.

Our approach combines established post-quantum primitives (NIST-standardized algorithms) with innovative protocol design to create practical, privacy-preserving systems that will protect your data for decades to come.

Key Principles

  • Privacy by Default - All transactions are private, no transparent mode
  • No Trusted Setup - All parameters are publicly verifiable
  • Transparency - Open specifications and future open-source implementations
  • AI-Verifiable - Specifications designed for autonomous implementation by advanced AI
  • Conservative Security - When in doubt, choose the more secure option
