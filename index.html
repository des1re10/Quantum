About This Project

Quantum is Phexora's research initiative focused on developing cryptographic protocols that remain secure against quantum computer attacks.

Our approach combines established post-quantum primitives (NIST-standardized algorithms) with innovative protocol design to create practical, privacy-preserving systems.

Key Principles

  • No Trusted Setup - All parameters are publicly verifiable
  • Transparency - Open specifications and future open-source implementations
  • AI-Verifiable - Specifications designed for autonomous implementation by advanced AI
  • Conservative Security - When in doubt, choose the more secure option
